Cosmic Laws

These are the foundational harmonics of reality—laws that govern all planes, all beings, all timelines. They are not commandments, but currents. To know them is to navigate the multiverse with clarity and grace.

🌐 Law of Divine Unity

All is one. Every particle, person, and plane is interconnected. Separation is illusion; unity is the underlying truth.

🧠 Law of Mentalism

All is mind. Consciousness precedes form. The universe is a mental construct shaped by thought and awareness.

🎵 Law of Vibration

Everything moves, everything vibrates. Frequency determines form, emotion, and experience.

🔁 Law of Correspondence

As above, so below. As within, so without. Patterns repeat across scales and dimensions.

⚖️ Law of Polarity

Everything has its opposite. Light and dark, joy and sorrow—each defines and completes the other.

⚧️ Law of Gender

Masculine and feminine energies exist in all things. Creation requires balance and interplay between these forces.

🌊 Law of Rhythm

All flows in cycles. Expansion and contraction, inhale and exhale—rhythm governs movement and change.

🔥 Law of Causality

Every action has a reaction. Cause and effect ripple through time and space, shaping outcomes.

🌀 Law of Relativity

All truths are contextual. Meaning arises through comparison, contrast, and perspective.

⚖️ Law of Balance

Equilibrium is the natural state. When extremes arise, the universe seeks restoration.

💰 Law of Compensation

Energy given is energy returned. Every gift, sacrifice, or effort finds its echo.

🎶 Law of Resonance

Like attracts like. Vibrations harmonize with matching frequencies, drawing similar energies together.

🌟 Law of Attraction

Focused thought magnetizes reality. What you dwell on, you draw toward you.

🔄 Law of Perpetual Transmutation

Energy is always in motion. Lower frequencies can be elevated; stagnation can be transformed.

📜 Closing Reflection

These laws are not static—they are alive. Study them, feel them, apply them. Let them tune your awareness and guide your path through the multidimensional weave.
